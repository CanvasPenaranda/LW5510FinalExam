Short Narrative of the Movie:

In the movie Crimson Tide, CAPT Ramsey (Gene Hackman) is the Commander of the submarine, USS Alabama, a ballistic nuclear submarine. Lieutenant Commander Ron Hunter (Denzel Washington) reports for duty to replace the Executive Officer who is out due to a bout with appendicitis. The USS Alabama receives orders to provide support for a rebel uprising in Russia. The rebels have taken control of Russian military bases that house nuclear weapons pointed at the United States, they also control the launch coder for these weapons. The USS Alabama is assigned to be prepared to deploy pre-emptive strikes against the Rebels before they can launch the missiles aimed at the United States. As the situation with the Rebels worsens, the USS Alabama receives a preliminary execution order and begins launch preparations.

As this scenario is developing, USS Alabama engages with a hostile Russian Submarine, USS Alabama suffers an indirect hit during the exchange. The radio communication is damaged as the crew is receiving a second message to either confirm the missile launch or abort the initial order. When CAPT Ramsey and LCDR Hunter disagree on how to handle the matter, the movie plot begins
